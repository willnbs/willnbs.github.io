Research Bio

My research interests revolve around the use of groundwater in energy, minerals and mining. This interest include the following: (i) integrating energetic uses for groundwater and ground-source (geothermal) energy in energy system models such as ongoing work with oemof (open energy modelling framework); (ii) sustainable groundwater resource management in the mining and energy sectors through the development of international reporting standards; (iii) mapping of system dynamics of social-ecological systems with direct application to groundwater.
