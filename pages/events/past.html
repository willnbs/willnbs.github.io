On track for Net Zero? Perspectives on UK's Evolving Energy Policy Landscape

Joint online roundtable with external connections

Location: Online

Abstract:

This roundtable was a collaboration between early career research networks, addressing how the UK's energy policy landscape is evolving to meet net-zero targets. With the introduction of initiatives such as the Clean Power 2030 Action Plan 2024, and the establishment of Great British Energy, it is essential to understand the key priorities for energy sector transformation and clearly identify the key stakeholders, whose collaboration is crucial to driving the transition.

About ResearchNet

The Early Career Researcher Network is a platform connecting researchers, fostering collaboration, and accelerating scientific discovery across all disciplines. Our mission is to break down barriers and enable research that addresses sustainable energy challenges.
